Public Access

California contains an abundance of water data however, because of the fragmented nature of the data, many datasets remain in silos and cannot be easily discovered and combined for potentially beneficial use-cases such as comparative analysis across states and countries, innovation, and scientific discovery.

Opportunities

Accessibility of water data can be improved by providing visualization platforms, in addition to the raw data. This will allow an audience with a wider range of data manipulation skills to gain meaningful insights from the available data.
