Public Access

On 23 September, 2016, the California State Assembly passed The Open and Transparent Water Data Act. The act requires the Department of Water Resources to establish a public benefit corporation that would create and manage a statewide water information accounting system to improve the ability of the state to meet the growing demand for water supply reliability and healthy ecosystems. The establishment of such a system requires protocols on data sharing, quality control, public access to the system, and promotion of open source platforms and decision support tools
