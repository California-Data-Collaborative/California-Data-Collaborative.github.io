Opportunities

The California Data Collaborative has created a standard protocol for water rate information and is in the process of converting inconsistent rate structures into its Open Water Rate Specification format. The creation of a comprehensive protocol to water rates is the first step in its adoption across water districts much liike the story of Google Transit Feed Specification.
